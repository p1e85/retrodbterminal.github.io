HOW YOUR DATA IS SAVED

This website is designed with your privacy as the top priority.

1. Purely Local Storage: Everything you type is saved directly in your web browser's local storage. No data is ever sent to or stored on an external server.

2. Developer Invisibility: Because your data never leaves your machine, we (the developers) cannot see, access, or share your notes. Your entries are completely private to you.

3. Browser Dependent: Your saved list is tied to the browser you are currently using. If you clear your browser's data or switch to a different device, your list will not be available there. The downloaded `.txt` files are your permanent backup.
